Dear Students, Faculty and Staff,
Despite the ways COVID-19 has disrupted every aspect of our lives, I have never been prouder to be a member of this community. I am truly grateful for your patience, passion and perseverance. I hope you will take a few minutes to watch the video below to allow me to express my sincere appreciation.
I hope you will also congratulate our #ILLINOIS2020 graduates. Join us to celebrate everything they have accomplished by visiting go.illinois.edu/celebration at noon on Saturday, May 16.
Thank you for your creativity and your commitment to the health and well-being of our university community. And I want to especially commend those who have continued to deliver essential services on-site during this time. Our hearts are with the people whose family, friends and loved ones have been affected in this pandemic.
Though we are separated by distance, we are always here together in Illinois spirit.
Sincerely,Robert J. JonesChancellor
